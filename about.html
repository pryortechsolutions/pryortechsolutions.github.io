ABOUT US

We have a simple philosophy:

We believe in supporting local first, with passion and fairness in all that we do. We strive to bring quality and craftsmenship to all aspects of our business and our clients.



OUR HISTORY

Founded in 2022 by Colby Pryor, his goal was to provide affordable and local IT services to residents and small businesses. After working in big corporate, Colby noticed that the local market is oftentimes neglected and because of this they are often stuck advancing their business. After a hiatus, Colby tooks the skills and ideas he formed over the two years to restart Pryor Technical Solutions with a new approach: local first, always.


Since then, Colby and others have served clients across the province of Newfoundland & Labrador in many different industries. Today, Colby focuses on providing residential and business IT solutions to everyone in the Conception Bay South Area.
